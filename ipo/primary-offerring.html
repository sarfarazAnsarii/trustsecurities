Help

For ASBA applications:

  1. Pre-filled application form will be emailed to the Clients registered email id post bidding of IPO application.
  2. You can take printout of the application form and check all the details as appearing in the application form i.e. Applicant Name, PAN, Demat, Bank, Bank Branch, category, sub-category, series and amount.
  3. Put Bid Number at the top of the application form & submit the application form to the Bank Location selected at the time of online application bidding.
  4. If all the details are correct in the application form, please sign [signatures – 5A/5B/5C for First/Second/Third Applicant & 5D for ASBA Bank account holder(s)].
  5. If any details are in-correct or wish to modify / cancel the bid, kindly send the email on trustsecurities@trustgroup.in alongwith the IPO bidding details from the email id which was captured while submission of bidding.

For UPI Applications:

  1. No need to submit physical application anywhere if mode of payment is selected as UPI.
  2. Request you to accept / authorise blocking of funds as per request received from UPI handle for completion of IPO bidding
